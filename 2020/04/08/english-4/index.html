定语从句中的连词分为两类

有成分，有含义(4个)
  • that 做主/宾，指人/物

This is the program that/which is interesting.

  • which 做主/宾，指物

This is the program that/which is interesting.

  • who 做主，指人

Du is person that/who likes shoes.

  • whom 做宾，指人

Du is person that/whom I admire.

有成分，有含义的连词的注意事项
只能用that的情况
  • （1）先行词为指物的不定代词:all-，every-，any-，some-，no-

You can choose anything that you like.

All the things that your ordered are here.

  • （2） 先行词被绝对含义的词修饰
    • 序数词
      The first thing that attracted me was your smile.
    • 最高级
      This was the happiest moment that happened in my life.
    • the only 唯一
    • the last 恰好
    • the very 最后
      Better days is the only film that l like.
  • (3)先行词既有人又有物

The kids and their behaviors that I saw shocked me.

不能用that的情况
  • (1)在非限定性定语从句中，有逗号，无that
  • (2)介词+连词做宾语的情况下，指物用which,指人用whom


The board on which they stood was frightening.

The girl with whom you talked just now is my friend.

无成分，有含义(4个)
  • why 为什么

I can’t forget the reason why I chose CS.

  • when 何时

I still remember the first day when I came to CS

  • where 何地

CS is the city where I have stayed for four years

  • whose 谁的

The milk tea whose name is CYYS is delicious.

无成分，有含义的连词的注意事项
介词+which的两种情况

方法：
将先行词带到从句中，从句与先行词用哪个介词连接，就填哪个介词。

  • （1）介词+which替代when where why

原句： I still remember the first day when I came to CS.

改写后：I still remember the first day on which I came to CS.

  • （2）介词+which固定用法(根据含义)

This is the book on which I spent 100 RMB.

非限制性定语从句中的固定句式

名/代/数 of+which/whom….
先行词是物用which
先行词是人用whom

In this movie there are many character,2 of whom are animals.
The main character is a cat, the name of which is Garfield.
A lot of people, most of whom are children like the movie.

补充
  • 定语从句中，连词作宾语可以省略(that which whom)
  • 学会区分定语从句与同位语从句

The reason why he didn’t come was that he was injured. 定语从句

I hope that the suggestion that I have offered dose good to them all.
